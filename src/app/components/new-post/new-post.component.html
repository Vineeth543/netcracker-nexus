Tips on getting good answers quickly

Make sure your question has not been asked already. Use the search function to find similar questions. If you don’t find your answer, you can ask a new one.

Steps

  • Summarize your problem in a one-line title.
  • Describe your problem in more detail.
  • Review your question and post it to the site.
Be specific and imagine you’re asking a question to another person. Include any error messages, key APIs, or unusual circumstances.
